Get involved, every little bit helps!

Volunteer

Sign up to work an evening shift at your local homeless shelter. Answer phones, serve food, wash dishes, distribute clothes, babysit kids, clean floors, fix a leaky toilet. Find out what they need and how you can help.

Educate yourself

There are myriad reasons why a person becomes homeless: lack of affordable housing, loss of a job, divorce, illness, substance abuse, domestic abuse, et cetera. Try to understand how they got there in the first place.

Advocate

Call your local homeless shelters to find out what is needed, then contact local scout troops to organize food drives or other fundraising events to pull those items together. Follow local politics and speak up!

Listen to Their Stories

Talk to them and hear their stories. Invite a homeless person to your local community event. Organize an outing to take homeless kids to the movies or an ice skating rink. Offer moral support when you can.

Job Fairs

Encourage your church or community center to have job fairs to help a homeless person find any work that might fit. Many homeless people want to work but can't find regular employment.

Join The Fight

Join the fight against homelessness. Fill out the quick form, become a part of our team and start actively participating in upcoming events. You can make us come one step closer to saying Homeless No More!
