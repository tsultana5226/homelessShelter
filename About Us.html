H.H.C. at a Glance

Homeless Hospitality Centre focuses on helping those who have been displaced transition into permanent housing. We have two unique services aimed at helping the homeless feel welcomed and loved, aimed at helping create a smooth and easy transition into permanent housing.

What Do We Provide?

Overnight Shelter

The Homeless Hospitality Centre provides up to 60 beds to make sure that everyone has a place to sleep at night. The centre welcomes everyone in need.

Daytime Hospitality Centre

We pride ourselves in making sure we provide everything to make our members get back on their feet. This would range from counseling to job searches.
